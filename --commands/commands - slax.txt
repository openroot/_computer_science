Assuming you just want to avoid typing
Code:
startx
every time, edit the file /etc/inittab and change the line that looks like
Code:
id:3:initdefault:
to
Code:
id:4:initdefault:
Many other distros use runlevel 5 for their graphical login. In Slackware, runlevel 5 is identical to runlevel 3 (ie console boot).

If new to Linux, think about leaving it as it is to fixing things easier in break something in the GUI.
